Numerica

Numerica type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

The foundations build up by rank - suits and colors are irrelevant. One card is flipped over at a time and moved onto one of four stacks. There are no restrictions on which card may go where on the stacks. Once on a stack, a card can only be moved onto a foundation.

Strategy

To solve this, you will need to plan carefully when placing the cards onto the stacks - one wrong move and you'll never be able to untangle the mess. A good player can win about one out of three games without taking back moves.
